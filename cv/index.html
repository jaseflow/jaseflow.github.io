Experience

  • LGIAsuper

    UX Developer (Nov 2020 - Jan 2021)

    I was contracted to LGIAsuper for three months to lead the design of the new Retirement Calculator. The development and financial modelling of the calculator was handled by a third party financial company. This made for an interesting task of making sure LGIAsuper was satifised with the look and feel of the calculator while also making sure I was working within the parameters set by the financial company.

    Highlights

    • Designed a mobile first calculator that asks members for their current financial situation (majority pre-filled), their required income in retirement and their future financial goals then calculates the age they will be able to retire with the income they chose.
    • Created a design library for the calculator in Figma
    • Created Figma components library for calculator and used this to compose mobile mocks to present to leaders at LGIAsuper
    • Created and hosted a fully functional React prototype to explore the design and undertook internal user testing around the office
    • Designed for larger screens in the browser and then presented individually with stakeholders
    • Wrote a user test plan for Account Managers to use when running moderated user testing on members. These sessions were recorded and I used the fotoage to iterate the design.
    • Worked closely with compliance and copywriters for sign off

  • Expedia

    Senior UX Designer (2016 - Mar 2020)

    Initially I was tasked with undoing the duplication penalties Google had applied to the templated brand langing pages.

    I then moved on to the destination content space where I worked with external content providers and SEO experts to create engaging and inspiring Destination Travel Guides .

    Highlights

    • Lead the design for the Expedia travel guide pages. These landing pages are built at scale, providing informative content on cities and neighborhoods all around the world.
    • Faciliate UX workshops, specifically "Design Studios", with multiple teams and projects.
    • Hypothesise and design UI tests with a focus on improving customer engagement metrics.
    • These tests resulted in an 8% improvement to conversion from our landing pages and a 15% increase to the number of visitors entering the shopping path.
    • Maintained and contributed new features to Expedia's design system. My contributions were design changes as well as the technical implementation.
    • Increased overall SEO traffic to our landing pages by 25% over a 12 month period. This was achieved by designing a network of hub and spoke landing pages that maintained a balance of engaging content for the users and rich crawlable content for Google.
    • Run CSS workshops to help up-skill backend developers and less technical designers.
    • Create and maintain a NodeJS/React prototyping app to which was used to perform user testing on real customers in the lab. This app was also used for design showcases, a live visual reference for engineers and as a tool for sharing concepts with stakeholders.
    • Work closely with backend developers to design microservices and help improve APIs for the frontend.
